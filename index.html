Marilyn Monroe

An American pop culture icon

Here's a timeline of her life:

  • 1926: Born Norma Jeane Mortenson
  • 1942: First marriage to Jimmy Dougherty
  • 1945: First modeling shoot with Andre de Dienes
  • 1946: Signs first contract with 20th Century Fox (as Marilyn Monroe)
  • 1950: Asphalt Jungle is released
  • 1953: Gentlemen Prefer Blondes and How to Marry a Millionaire are released
  • 1954: Second marriage to Joe DiMaggio
  • 1955: The Seven Year Itch is released
  • 1956: Third marriage to Arthur Miller
  • 1959: Some Like it Hot is released
  • 1962: Dies at age 36
